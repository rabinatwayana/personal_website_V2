Description

The course consist of following two module:

Business Strategy: This module focused on how to pitch a venture based on the stage of your journey. It covered six key components of a successful pitch. The core idea of developing a business strategy and achieving success involves having a clear understanding of your customers, the problems they face, and how your product addresses those issues. Additionally, startups should have a future-oriented perspective, highlighting whether the problem is growing and if new customers will emerge. The module also emphasized the ways to create more value through your solutions.

Business Model: It discussed on Nine blocks of Business Model Canvas that would help and guide in launching new or innovative start-up.
