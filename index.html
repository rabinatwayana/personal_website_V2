ABOUT MYSELF


I am a geomatics engineering graduate from Kathmandu University,Nepal. Presently, I am working as a WebGIS Developer at NAXA Pvt. Ltd, Kathmandu, Nepal. I am currently gathering professional experience in the WebGIS sector since last two years. Whilst, I also have sound knowledge in the geoinformatics areas such as; RS, UAV technology, GIS, FOSS, Geospatial Programming, etc. I am enthusiast about the integration of Web GIS technologies along with other geoinformatics tools for the improvement of critical aspects of natural resources and other physical infrastructural aspects. Currently, exploring how Web GIS and other geoinformatics tools can support in eradicating the current environmental, rural road network, and drinking water issues in the context of the vulnerable communities of Nepal as well as in Africa. I also actively involved in publishing papers in journals and conference proceedings in the sector of WebGIS and geoinformatics. Despite all, I had very good academic track records and holds numerous academic excellence awards.
